About Z&D Airsoft

Veteran-Owned. Family-Focused. Built for Safety, Driven by Purpose.

🇺🇸 Veteran-Owned: Founded by Staff Sergeant Mark Ellis, USMC (Ret.), Z&D Airsoft brings military discipline and leadership to every event.
❤️ We Give Back: 10% of our gross profits go to local charities every quarter. Community is at the heart of what we do.

Z&D Airsoft, LLC is a mobile airsoft and Nerf experience based in Findlay, Ohio. We specialize in bringing high-energy entertainment to schools, festivals, and private parties — all with a laser-focus on safety and structured fun.

Mark’s 10+ years of active duty service — including deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan — shape the professionalism and care we bring to every mission. We also proudly support local causes through quarterly charitable donations.

🔒 Core Principles

  • Safety First: Safety briefings, eye protection, and no PvP combat
  • All Ages Welcome: Nerf (6+), Airsoft (8+, <13 w/ supervision)
  • Veteran-Led: Structure, discipline, and passion in every event
  • Community Driven: 10% donations to local causes, every quarter
