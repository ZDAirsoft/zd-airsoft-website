Giving Back: Our Mission

At Z&D Airsoft, we believe in more than just tactical fun — we believe in purpose. Each quarter, 10% of our gross profits are donated to charities making a difference in our community. From veterans’ services to youth programs, we’re proud to stand behind causes that align with our core values of leadership, responsibility, and service.

🏆 This Quarter's Recipient

Hancock County Veterans Services

In Q2 2025, we proudly donated $300 to Hancock County Veterans Services, supporting local veterans with housing assistance, transportation, and community outreach.

🔜 Next Quarter's Focus

Children’s Mentoring Connection of Hancock County

Our next campaign will support CMC’s youth mentorship programs — helping shape tomorrow’s leaders with guidance, confidence, and opportunity.
