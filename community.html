Giving Back: Our Mission

At Z&D Airsoft, we believe in more than just tactical fun — we believe in purpose. Each quarter, 10% of our gross profits are donated to charities making a difference in our community. From veterans’ services to youth programs, we’re proud to stand behind causes that align with our core values of leadership, responsibility, and service.

🕰️ Last Quarter’s Recipient (Q1 2025)

Findlay City Mission

In Q1 2025, we donated $250 to Findlay City Mission to support shelter, meals, and life-stabilization programs for individuals and families in need throughout Hancock County.

🏆 Current Recipient (Q2 2025)

Findlay City Schools

This quarter, we’re proud to support Findlay City Schools by donating funds to help provide educational resources and supplies for the upcoming school year.

🎁 Annual Commitment: Q3 – Toys for Tots

Toys for Tots

Every Q3, our donation is reserved for the Marine Corps' Toys for Tots program — a cause that’s deeply personal to us. Founder Mark Ellis served as a Toys for Tots Coordinator during his time in the U.S. Marine Corps, and we are honored to continue supporting this mission of bringing joy to children in need.
