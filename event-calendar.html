Pricing & Booking Options

Flexible Packages for Every Type of Event

Z&D Airsoft brings the mission to you with safe, structured airsoft and Nerf gameplay—perfect for private parties, school field days, community festivals, and corporate events.

🎯 Pay-to-Play Model (Ideal for Public Events & Festivals)

  • $5 – 1 Magazine (Approx. 5 minutes or 100 BBs)
  • $10 – 3 Magazines (Up to 10 minutes) – Best Value!
  • Safe, quick, and fun for individuals or families
  • Revenue sharing or vendor fees available depending on event policies

🎉 Flat-Rate Private Event Packages

  • Small Event (Up to 50 participants): $300 for 3 hours + $50/hr extra
  • Medium Event (50–100 participants): $400 for 4 hours + $50/hr extra
  • Large Event (100+ participants): $500 for 5 hours + $50/hr extra
  • Accommodates up to 36 participants per hour (5-minute rotations)
