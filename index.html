🎒 Back to School Bash – Texas Roadhouse, Findlay

Date: Monday, August 11th, 2025 – 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM

Location: 1111 W Trenton Ave, Findlay, OH 45840

Join us at the Findlay Texas Roadhouse for their annual Back to School Bash — a fun-filled family event supporting local causes and helping kids head into the school year with a smile! Z&D Airsoft will be onsite with our fully enclosed mobile Nerf & Airsoft range, offering arcade-style smart target games, safety instruction, and high-energy fun for ages 6+.

  • 🎯 Free to attend | Pay-to-Play
  • 💥 All safety gear provided
  • 🔁 Alternating Nerf & Airsoft rounds
  • 🎪 Inflatables, games, food trucks & more!

💡 Proceeds will help support local community programs — including a percentage of Z&D Airsoft’s revenue. Come out, have fun, and give back!
