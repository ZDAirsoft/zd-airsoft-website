About Us

Veteran-Owned. Family-Focused. Built for Safety, Driven by Purpose.

Z&D Airsoft, LLC is a mobile airsoft and Nerf experience based in Findlay, Ohio, designed to deliver high-energy fun with an unwavering commitment to safety, responsibility, and community values.

Founded by (Fmr.) U.S. Marine Corps Staff Sergeant Mark Ellis, Z&D Airsoft was born from a passion for structured, purpose-driven training environments—combined with a love for family-friendly entertainment. Mark brings over 10 years of active duty service, including deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan, as well as Marine Corps-level Rifle and Pistol Expert Marksmanship credentials.

His experience leading Marines and serving as a Coordinator for Toys for Tots inspired him to build something unique: a safe environment that teaches responsibility, while also demonstrating community responsibility through our commitment to donate locally, ensuring we make an impact on our neighbors.

Our trailer comes equipped with tethered airsoft and Nerf blasters, smart targets, and a safety-focused team. From birthdays to fairs, we bring the mission to you—no power needed.

Our Core Principles

  • Safety First: Mandatory safety briefings, eye protection, and no PvP combat.
  • All Ages Welcome: Nerf (6+), Airsoft (8+, under 13 require parental supervision), sessions alternate for comfort.
  • Community Driven: 10% of our gross profits go to a local charity every quarter.
  • Veteran-Led: Professionalism, structure, and leadership in every event.
